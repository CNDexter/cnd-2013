About

Alpha Sigma Alpha is a national collegiate sorority whose mission is thus:

"[We are] a women's organization that exists to promote high ideals and standards for its members throughout their lives by emphasizing balance among the four aims of intellectual, physical, spiritual and social development. Alpha Sigma Alpha provides structure and guidance through:

Sisterhood, which is a strong bond of unconditional friendship based on common values and experiences;

Heritage, which is expressed through creed, ritual and history;

Opportunities for leadership and involvement in chapter, campus and community."

Nu Nu chapter is no exception as the chapter consists of intelligent, extroverted, vivacious, and purposeful women who strive for a well-rounded college experience.
