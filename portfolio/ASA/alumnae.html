Alumnae

Alpha Sigma Alpha considers our alumnae to be an important part of our organization. Therefore we try to hold as many events with them as possible. We realize that many of our sister move away after graduation to begin their own lives and getting together isn't quite as easy as it once was. This is why we are exploring new avenues of communication to keep in touch with as many sisters as possible.

We are starting up a newsletter specifically targeted to our alumnae to keep them knowledgeable about the chapter happenings and to keep them updated on how other alumnae are doing. We hope to eventually have more available to our sisters to increase communication. If you would like to be included in the list of sisters to receive this newsletter please email NuNuAlum@gmail.com.
