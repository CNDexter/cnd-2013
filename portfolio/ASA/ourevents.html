Our Events

We at Nu Nu chapter have several events throughout the year that we consider to be part of our tradition. Some of these include Bid Day, Senior Dinner, Ladybug Sleepovers, etc. Within the last year we have taken the initiative and started our Greek Calendar. We hope to continue this event well into the future and establish it as our one traditional event.

Greek Calendar

The Greek Calendar is a calendar that a number of sisters put together for the then upcoming 2007 year. The calendar is not a new concept to Nu Nu as sisters from previous years have done it before. However we're trying revive that tradition and make it an annual event once again. The 2007 calendar was composed of twelve men from the IFC fraternities on campus. The 2008 calendar will feature both men and women from our fellow sororities and fraternities. We sell the calendar to the neighboring sororities and fraternities, parents, alumni, as well as to the non-affiliated Drexel community.
