    Greenphire CEO to Chair panel at the 2009 DIA Annual Conference

    PHILADELPHIA (March 4, 2009) - greenphire CEO, Samuel Whitaker, has been selected to Chair a session entitled Increasing Enrollment, Retention, and Compliance through Electronic Patient Payment and Communication Technologies at the 45th Annual DIA Conference. The panel will review current strategies to drive enrollment, retention and compliance and will explore the effects of new technology-driven strategies. New technologies including the combination of electronic payment and communication technologies, such as those leveraged in greenphire's products, will be discussed and examined.

    The DIA 45th Annual Meeting will be held June 21 - 25, 2009, in San Diego, California, and the panel will take place June 24, 2009 from 1:30 - 3:00PM.

    GreenPhire featured in PharmaVOICE

    PHILADELPHIA (February 2009) - greenphire, Inc. along with CEO Samuel Whitaker was featured in PharmaVOICE a leading pharmaceutical magazine. The article Process Improvements Investigator Payments reviews the challenges that revolve around investigator payments and highlights the need for new technologies that are specifically designed for the site and the investigator.
