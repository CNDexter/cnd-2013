1. How does a ClinCard compare to a check?

The ClinCard is far better than a check. Instead of going to a bank and waiting days for a check to clear, or going to a check-cashing facility and paying a hefty check-cashing fee, the ClinCard provides patients with immediate access to their money without charging a banking or check-cashing fee. Patients don’t have to worry about losing their ClinCard because their funds will be returned to them. A ClinCard provides the patient with 100% security in the event of loss or theft, and the ability to use their funds in millions of stores and ATM locations worldwide, as well as on the Internet.

2. When do patients have access to their funds?

Patients have access to their funds within 1 business day after receiving their ClinCard. Patients can leave the trial site and go directly to a store or ATM and access their funds. The ClinCard is accepted at millions of ATM and store locations around the world.

3. What happens if a patient loses his or her ClinCard?

Patients may receive a new card at their next visit or receive a replacement card by calling customer service. If a card is lost or stolen, all of the patient’s funds are 100% secured. A complimentary replacement card will be provided to each patient.

4. Will the ClinCard improve patient retention and compliance?

Yes. Not only does the ClinCard give patients a faster and more convenient way to receive their reimbursement, but the ClinCard also introduces several dynamic communication tools to help significantly reduce patient drop-out rates. Each of these communication tools is customized and structured by greenphire for the individual needs of each trial.

5. How does greenphire keep patients thinking about the study?

The ClinCard uses customized and personalized text messaging, email messaging, a fully customizable cardholder website, a custom designed plastic, and customizable print communications that are included in the fully customizable card package that patients receive when they initially receive their ClinCard.

6. Why are these communication tools important?

Communicating with a patient throughout the entire length of the trial is critical to keeping a patient actively involved. greenphire’s communication and payment strategy reduces patient loss to follow-up and non-compliance. Our communication tools help the patient stay informed about the trial and the reimbursement payments that they receive during their participation. The ClinCard helps to leverage the reimbursement payment in order to create a positive association for the patient with the trial every time the patient sees or uses their ClinCard.

7. How can greenphire’s analytical reporting tools add value?

greenphire’s analytical reporting tools are essential to management at the sponsor level. Analytical reports enable trial administrators to understand enrollment, retention, and financial statistics across all trial sites, on demand. Reporting is especially useful in evaluating and improving site selection for future trials, and understanding the value added to the trial by the ClinCard.

8. How quickly can greenphire implement a ClinCard for my trial?

greenphire provides each client with a dedicated implementation consultant who designs and customizes the ClinCard for each specific trial. Implementation times vary depending on the complexity of the trial, but greenphire’s goal is to have all ClinCard programs into production within 30 days of a kickoff meeting.

9. Can I work with greenphire outside of the United States?

Beginning in 2009, greenphire will launch its European communication and payments platform. Our clients will be able to manage all US and European communications and payments through a single, centralized source. Following our European launch, greenphire will work to bring online other key international geographies in order to help our clients improve their clinical trial worldwide.
