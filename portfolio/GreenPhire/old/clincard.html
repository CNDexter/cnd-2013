ClinCard: Innovating Clinical Trials

The ClinCard improves the patient’s trial experience by reinventing the patient’s reimbursement payment. greenphire leverages its payment technology to introduce proactive communication tools, on-demand analytical reporting and centralized financial auditing functionality. Each feature of the ClinCard is fully customizable for each trial and is designed to benefit the patient while increasing retention, increasing compliance, and providing a powerful point of control.
