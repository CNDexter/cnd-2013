Who is greenphire?

greenphire was inspired by the unique background of its principals and has grown from their careers in the finance, technology, pharmaceutical, communication, and payments industries.

greenphire fully understands the impact of increasing patient retention, increasing patient compliance, and innovating the administration of clinical trials. At the same time, greenphire has paid specific attention to creating a product that provides a superior and seamless user experience for the most important contributors to the clinical trials, the patients.

greenphire has combined its proprietary payments and communication platforms to unite prepaid debit card technology and proactive real-time communication tools, resulting in the creation of the ClinCard product. The fully branded, secure, and customizable features of the ClinCard product include network branded prepaid debit cards, secure text messaging for appointment and compliance reminders, automated email messages to communicate trial information, and the myClinCard.com website, which acts as the patient’s online banking and information tool.

For the patient, greenphire represents immediate access to funds, absolutely no check-cashing fees, 100% security of funds, payment flexibility, convenient trial reminders, and 24/7/365 customer service.

For our clients, greenphire represents reduced costs, operational efficiencies, centralized auditing, real-time reporting and analytics, and the ability to increase patient retention.
