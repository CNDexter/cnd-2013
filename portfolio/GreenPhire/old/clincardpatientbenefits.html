Patient Benefits: Improving the Patient's Experience

1. Immediate Payment – The ClinCard gives patients immediate access to their reimbursement payment. Previously, patients would have to travel to a bank or check-cashing facility and wait up to 5 days for their funds to be made available to them. A patient can leave a research site and go directly to an ATM or a local store and access funds.

2. No Fees to Access Your Money – Cashing a check can cost a patient up to 10% of the face value of the check. With a ClinCard, patients can access their money without paying a fee. Patients now have immediate access to 100% of their reimbursement payment.

3. 100% Security – If a patient's ClinCard is lost or stolen, 100% of the lost or stolen funds may be reimbursed to the patient.

4. Flexible & Convenient – The ClinCard can be used at any of the millions of ATM and store locations worldwide.

5. Online Banking – Patients may log on to www.myClinCard.com and track all deposits and withdrawal transactions that are associated with their ClinCard. A ClinCard is especially beneficial to patients who do not have a bank account or a credit card. These patients now have the ability to make card purchases that they were unable to make in the past.

6. Appointment & Compliance Reminders – Through www.myClinCard.com patients are reminded of upcoming appointments, trial requirements, financial information, and other health-related information. This resource provides the patient with a customized online tool to learn about trial related information that is important to them.

7. Financial Notifications – Each time a payment is loaded onto a patient’s ClinCard, patients will receive a text message notification confirming that they have access to the new funds. Patients will also receive periodic reminders to let them know the balance on their ClinCard. Proactive messaging helps make sure that patients utilize the entire amount that has been reimbursed to them.

8. Customer Service – The patient has access to customer service (IVR and a live CS representative) 24/7/365. Anytime a question arises about a patient's ClinCard, a live representative will be available to answer the patient's questions. The patient may also submit customer service inquiries through the www.myClinCard.com website or by email.
