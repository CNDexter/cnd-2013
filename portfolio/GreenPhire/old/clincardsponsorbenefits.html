Client Benefits: Creating an Efficient and Reliable Trial

1. Increased Retention and Compliance – By improving the patient payment process the overall patient experience is enhanced. Each time patients use their ClinCard, they are reminded of their participation in the trial. Additionally, the patient is proactively reminded of upcoming appointments and protocol requirements through proactive text messaging, email messaging, and through the www.myClinCard.com website.

2. More Effective Communication Tools – The ClinCard product introduces new communication tools to keep the patient actively connected to the trial after leaving the research site. greenphire’s system delivers appointment reminders, compliance reminders, financial notifications, and other health-related information to the patient throughout the length of the protocol via text messages, email messages, and through the www.myClinCard.com website. Direct electronic communication with the patient significantly reduces both patient loss to missed follow-up (up to 40%), and patient non-compliance (up to 80%).

3. Custom Branded ClinCard – Clients may fully customize the design of the ClinCard to carry the branding of their choice. Each time patients use their card, they are reminded of the clinical trial, their next appointment, and the requirements of the trial. The ClinCard acts as a “Billboard in Your Wallet,” creating a positive reminder of the trial every time the card is used.

4. Full Product Customization – Each ClinCard program is fully customizable, so our clients are able to custom design each feature and function to be trial-specific. The card, card package, and www.myClinCard.com website can carry all the branding and messaging that a client wants to associate with each trial. All messaging tools are customizable in terms of technical delivery structure as well as content. greenphire provides a technical implementation team to each client in order to customize the ClinCard solution to meet the specific requirements of each trial.

5. On-Demand Enrollment and Retention Reports – Because reimbursement payments are usually linked to a patient’s site visits or other required activity, greenphire can provide real-time reporting that allows the client to track the number of patients enrolled, patient retention statistics, communication statistics, expected trial completion date, and the economic value that has been generated by the ClinCard product. greenphire can also provide analytical reports on both enrollment and retention on a site by site basis in order to help our clients analyze and understand individual site performance.

6. Simplified Financial Tracking – By centralizing the payments process, the client can track all reimbursement payments and other financial statistics, on demand, from a centralized source. greenphire provides each client with the ability to receive both standard and customizable reports. Our reporting team can work with our clients in order to make sure all necessary details are being provided in a format that is most convenient.

7. Improved Cash Flow at the Site Level – By transferring the financial responsibility of patient reimbursement away from the site, greenphire helps our clients to alleviate cash flow pain at the site level, creating a more effective operational environment for investigators.

8. International Capability – Patients who receive a ClinCard through a North American trial site may use their ClinCard at ATM and store locations outside of the US. In 2009, the ClinCard will also start to be made available to European patients, allowing multi-national trials to be coordinated through a single payment and communication resource.

9. Integrate with EDC Providers – greenphire is capable of integrating its payment and communication platform with a client’s preferred EDC and/or IVR provider in order to create a fully automated payments process.
