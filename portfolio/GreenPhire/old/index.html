greenphire is a communication and payment specialist that combines electronic communication tools with prepaid debit card technology to create the ClinCard, an unparalleled reimbursement tool for the clinical trials industry.

The ClinCard leverages a proprietary communication and payments platform to provide a fully customizable product that significantly increases patient retention and compliance. At the same time the ClinCard alleviates the pain associated with payment administration and site-level cash flow shortages. greenphire’s platform also enables trial managers to receive real-time reporting and analytics on trial performance within a multi-site environment.

For patients, the ClinCard provides a payment vehicle that is more convenient, more secure, more flexible, and more economical, while introducing real-time and proactive appointment reminders, compliance tips, balance updates, and healthcare information.
