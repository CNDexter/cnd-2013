    ClinCard
    ReferralCard
    Investigator Payments

greenphire's ReferralCard is designed to increase the number of referrals (individuals prequalified for a study during the recruiting process) who actually attend the first site visit, consequently increasing the speed in which a study enrolls patients. The structure of the ReferralCard's features is designed:

  1. Drives referrals to take the initiative to contact the site and make their own first office appointment, removing reliance from the site coordinator.


  2. Maintains an ongoing and active line of communication between the study and the referral between the moment of prequalification and the attendance of the first site visit.


  3. Provides a structured delivery of travel reimbursement funds to the referral in order to help mitigate the cost of both travelling to and returning from the first site visit.
