Samuel Whitaker, Founder & CEO
samuel.whitaker@greenphire.com

Prior to founding greenphire, Sam was a Vice President in the Product Development group of Citigroup's prepaid card division. In this capacity, Sam was responsible for managing several existing products and developing new payment solutions for new and existing clients. Prior to working for Citigroup, Sam spent his career working in transactional finance both as an investment banker and as a member of the investment team of a Philadelphia based holding company. Sam earned a BA from the University of Pennsylvania.

John Samar, Founder & COO
john.samar@greenphire.com

Prior to founding greenphire, John was a Vice President in the Product Development group of Citigroup's prepaid card division. In this capacity, John was responsible for designing and developing internal operational applications that supported client programs and significantly improved operational efficiency and security. Prior to working for Citigroup, John worked as a structural analyst for Pagnotta Engineering, a boutique engineering firm specializing in aerospace related defense contracts. John earned a BS in Mechanical Engineering and an MS in Management Science from Lehigh University.

Dan Worth, VP of Engineering
dan.worth@greenphire.com

Dan is a seasoned technologist with strong leadership and business acumen. With over 10 years experience in Internet technology, Dan brings to greenphire a unique ability to match technology to business needs. Dan specializes in building internet/e-commerce platforms that have scalable architectures with a focus on security.

Dan began his career at Wingspan Technologies. During Dan's time at Wingspan, he led the development of several high profile projects, allowing Wingspan to continue growing while maintaining a positive relationship with clients.

Dan received a B.S. in Computer Information Systems from Bloomsburg University.
