Dr. Neil Rotherham, Executive Chairman
neil.rotherham@greenphire.com

In 1993 Neil co-founded ClinPhone, recognizing the opportunity to improve the clinical trials process through the implementation of telephone and web-based solutions. With over 20 years' experience in the medical and pharmaceutical industries, Neil brings to greenphire a wealth of expertise in key markets for the company's suite of products.

Neil qualified in medicine in 1986 and trained as a hospital physician before moving into the pharmaceutical industry to work on medicine development for Boots Pharmaceuticals, prior to founding ClinPhone.

Patrick Hughes, Director
patrick.hughes@greenphire.com

Patrick brings over 10 years' experience in the pharmaceutical industry to greenphire. Most recently, Patrick was the Senior VP of Sales and Marketing at ClinPhone where he was responsible for designing and executing ClinPhone's global marketing strategy. Patrick currently works with several early stage technology companies in the clinical trials space consulting on sales and marketing strategy.

David Reuter, Director
david.reuter@greenphire.com

David Reuter is a Partner with LLR Partners, a leading private equity firm based in Philadelphia. David joined LLR in 1999 and has 11 years of private equity and accounting experience. David specializes in investments in the Financial Services, Information Technology and Outsourced Business Services industries. David earned a BS from Lehigh University and is a CPA in Pennsylvania.

Jennifer Peters, Director
jennifer.peters@greenphire.com

Jennifer is a Regional Director of Business Development for Transperfect, a global communication services company. As a Business Development professional with Transperfect, Jennifer focuses on providing communication solutions and cross border strategies to CROs, sponsors, recruiting firms and clinical trials technology providers. Jennifer earned a BA and an MA from Pace University.
