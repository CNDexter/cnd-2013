Grace Gonglewski (pronounced gone-glef-ski) is an award-winning professional voice-over and stage actress in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

After graduating from the North Carolina School of the Arts with a BFA, Grace came to Philadelphia to accept the Dorothy Haas Fellowship as the first ever Acting Apprentice at the Walnut Street Theatre. Since then she has acted in roles Off-Broadway and Regionally at the Flea Theatre, The Arden, The Walnut, The Wilma, Philadelphia Theatre Company, Delaware Theatre Company, The Pennsylvania Shakespeare Festival, and has toured Ireland with Interact Theatre Company.

Along with acting on the stage Grace Gonglewski has been the voice of Alex's Lemonade Stand, Boscov's, David's Bridal, Stainmaster Carpet and many banks, hospitals and politicians around the country.
